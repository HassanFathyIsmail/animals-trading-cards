tiger

The tiger is the biggest species of the cat family.
Tigers are good swimmers and can swim up to 6 kilometres.
  • Scientific Name : Panthera tigris
  • Average Length : mals:3.9m-2.5m,females:2,8m-2m
  • Average Lifespan : 16-18 year
  • Habitat : tropical forests

It is most recognisable for its dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur
with a lighter underside.
It is an apex predator,
primarily preying on ungulates such as deer and wild boar.
